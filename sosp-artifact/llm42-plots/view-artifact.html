Artifact · Reference vs Reproduced Figures
How to read this page. The left column shows the reference figures from the paper (llm42-plots/reference/); the right column shows the figures reproduced by your run (llm42-plots/). Panels on the right that read “not generated yet” simply mean that figure has not been produced — run the matching run.sh (or run_all.sh) to fill it in. Every plot is loaded as its original PDF, sized to fit with no scrollbars. For best rendering, serve this folder (python3 -m http.server) and open view-artifact.html.
